Is There a Link Between Household Income and Behavioral Risk Factors?

In this D3 document, we explore certain associations between demographics and behavioral risk factors using survey data collected in 2014.

The Demographic data was sourced from the U.S. Census American FactFinder tool. The Behavioral Risk Factor data was sourced from the CDC's Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System.

Each marker represents an individual state in the USA. Click on any of the axis titles to explore the data.
